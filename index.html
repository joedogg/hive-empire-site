Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the game and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Hive Empire works completely offline. Your bees will continue producing honey and you'll receive your offline earnings when you return.

How do I prestige / migrate my swarm?

Once you've earned at least 1 million coins in total, the Swarm Migration option becomes available. This resets your colony but grants permanent Royal Jelly bonuses!

Is my progress saved?

Your game auto-saves every 30 seconds. Progress is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your device backed up via iCloud.

How do I unlock new honey tiers?

New honey tiers unlock automatically based on your lifetime earnings. Keep growing your empire to discover all 17 legendary varieties!
