Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Hive Empire ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Game Data: We store your game progress locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • In-game purchases and virtual currency
  • Achievements and unlocked content
  • Game settings and preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to use cloud backup features.

In-App Purchases

Hive Empire offers optional in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

Analytics

We may collect anonymized, aggregated analytics data to improve the game experience. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and includes:

  • General gameplay statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Feature usage patterns

Advertising

Hive Empire may display optional video advertisements in exchange for in-game rewards. If you choose to watch ads, third-party advertising networks may collect certain information in accordance with their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Hive Empire is designed to be family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game can be played without providing any personal information.

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent data loss. We do not transmit or store your personal game data on external servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your game data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Contact us with questions about your privacy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
